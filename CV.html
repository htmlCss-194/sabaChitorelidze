Biography

Mads Schülle

Date of birth: 30.04.1980

Work location: UK

Hey, I’m Mads, the Norwegian designer, born is Oslo, now based in London, UK. I’ve been on the field more than 20 years and worked on UI/UX pro level almost six years.

I’m happily married and a father of two taughers. I really cherrish the time with my family since my previous jobs has taken me avay. Now, more steady, I can really focus on details and offer the perfection and offer analysed and tested solutions to boost up conpanys online solutions - such as ecommerce and corporate sites, web and mobile apps.

I’m also into hiking and good cup of coffee, these are my getaways to perfect trip on my own, to collect ideas and inspiration. With all that, I always carry my small Fuji camera with me, to capture moments what nature has us to offer.
